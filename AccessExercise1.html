Queries.

1. Find all cameras that were previously owned by Arlene Francis. BTW, who is Arlene Francis? Google her.

2. Find all cameras manufactured in Australia or in Canada.

3. Find all customers who live in Sierra Madre and bought a Hasselblad (any model). What's Hasselblad? Google it.

4. Find all customers who live in Burbank and own a Lexus.

5. Find all orders greater than $1200.

6. Find all orders done after 9/9/2015.

7. Find all orders of Nikon D7100 sold by Carla.

8. Find all customers who live in South Pasadena and bought a Pentax (any model).

9. Find all customers who are older than 48, live in Glendale and were sold a camera by Narine.

10. Find all customers who drive a Pontiac.

11. Find all customers who bought a camera the previously belonged to Angela Sarafyan, was manufactured in Germany and was sold by Shake or Regina.

12. Find all customers who live in Pasadena, own a Pontiac, are older than 25, ordered a camera after February 5th 2014. Who was their Salesperson?

13. Find all salespeople who sold a camera previously owned by Hedy Lamarr and manufactured in Germany.

14. Find all customers from Sierra Madre who bought a Leica (of any model) from Valerie.

15. Find all customers from Arcadia who own a Mercedes (any model), are older than 21, bought a Pentax (any model) manufactured in Germany. Who was the salesperson?

16. Find all salespeople who sold a camera manufactured in Germany to customers who live in Pasadena or South Pasadena and drive an Audi.

17. Did Reby sell any camera manufactured in Australia and previously owned by Angela Sarafyan?

18. Find all the cameras previously owned by Barbara Eden and manufactured in Germany.

19. Did Shake sell any Hasselbad (any model) to customers in South Pasadena?
